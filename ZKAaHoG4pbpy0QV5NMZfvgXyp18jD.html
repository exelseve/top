Ghaңalyқtar Unity bastap 2019 GDC

Unity gh / e ghaңa fizika қozғaltқysh. Tіptі ekі : Bіrіnshі — Unity Physics, ol defoltnym, ashyқ bastapқy kody bar, C#. Ekіnshі — Havok Physics Microsoft, aқyly. Na habre bolady oқuғa stateyku.

Call of Duty: Mobile ghasady arnalғan Unity. Қazіrdің өzіnde ghүrіp ghatyr pre-tіrkeu, android үshіn.

Almatyda Unity bolady AR Foundation — bіrіkken API gheңіldetu үshіn әzіrleu AR қoldanbalar men oyyndar.

Ghaman emes anonsy musadan, dep үmіttenemіz ghaңalyқ әkelmeydі ghaңa lagam da kүtpegen gherlerde :)

Adaptive Performance da Bіrlіk. Ghaңa ficha ghұmys іsteytіn bolady bastap Samsung S10. Menің tүsіngenіm, "suret" bolady beyіmdeluge baylanysty қuat құrylғylar үshіn lagov.

Unity қoldaytyn bolady guglovskuyu oyyn platforma — Stadia. Oynauғa bolady bolady kez kelgen құrylғyda bolsa, teledidar ghәne yutuba. Tіptі semesіnі өzіnің shyғardy. Bұl ne ghәne қalay ol ghұmys іsteydі, ghәne habre. Eger қysқasha aytar bolsaқ — barlyғy da қuanyshty bastalmaғan edі.

Unity satyp aldy Graphine. Bұl әzіrleushі BOYYNShA vizualizaciya fakturalardyң kollekciya naқty uaқyt. Paydalanuғa mүmkіndіk beredі UHD teksturanyң, shekteuge, olardy oңtaylandyru ghүkteu uaқyty.

DOTS. Unity tartady, өz құraldary bіr-bіr tehnologiyasy, data-baғdarlanғan. Sonda ECS ghәne C# Job System Burst Compiler. Havok da bolady da.

Al men үshіn ekі ese tұrmauғa — ishodniki demki MegaCity. Razvlekaytesy.
